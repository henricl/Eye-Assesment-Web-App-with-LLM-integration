Welcome to the Eye Health Assistant. Before you continue, please read and acknowledge the following:

This assistant provides general eye-health information and visual-acuity estimates only. It is not a doctor and never gives diagnoses or prescriptions.

  • No name or personal identifiers are required.

  • This is a research prototype, not a certified medical device.

How it works

1. You describe your symptoms

The assistant listens for redness, pain, blurry vision, discharge, light sensitivity, or other changes.

2. It asks key clarifying questions

Covers onset, one/both eyes, pain level, injuries, contact lenses, and vision changes.

3. You receive careful, general guidance

No diagnoses or prescriptions. Just typical possibilities and when to seek urgent care.

Safety principles

No diagnosis or prescriptions

The assistant avoids medication names, dosages, or any diagnostic claims.

Red-flag escalation

Sudden vision loss, trauma, or severe pain triggers urgent-care recommendations.

Eye-only scope

Non-eye symptoms are politely redirected back to eye concerns.
